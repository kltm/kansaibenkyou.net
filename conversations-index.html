Even with a familiarity of the vocabulary and grammar of Kansai, it may be hard to understand conversations between speakers of the dialect. To help build towards this goal, we have created twelve conversations based on real conversations — each conversation includes an audio track in Kansai-ben and the text in Kansai-ben, standard Japanese, and English. Use the buttons to toggle between all of these.
