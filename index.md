# Kansaibenkyou.net

A learning resource for Kansai-ben (関西弁), the family of Japanese
dialects spoken in the Kansai region — Osaka, Kyoto, Hyogo, Nara,
Wakayama, Shiga. Authored by Keiko Yukawa.
